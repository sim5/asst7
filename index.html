Lab Escape

  • High Concept: Escape from the mad scientists!
  • Player Experience Goals: Tension to run away from the scientists.
  • Genre: Arcade
  • Game play: The play move with the arrow keys. When the player crash into the wall or touch the mad scientists, lives decrease.
  • Prototype goal: Dodging, moving
  • Features: The scientists keep chasing after the player until the end.
  • Setting/Premise: One day, a man got kidnapped and he is about to be a specimen for a human experiment. He woke up and found that mad scientists are surrounding him.
  • Story: A kidnapped guy tries to run away from the mad scientists. After 30 seconds, the gate opens and he can escape.
  • Target Audience: Any age group.
  • Play time: Around 30 seconds.
  • Strategies: The player should try to stay away from the scientists while not crashing into the wall.
